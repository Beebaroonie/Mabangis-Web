JUEGO TODO: THE EVOLUTION OF COMBAT

The Philosophy: "Anything Goes"

The term translates to "Anything Goes." Historically, it was the ultimate private proving ground a high-stakes environment where masters tested their deadliest skills in a fight with no limits. Today, Juego Todo has evolved. We have taken that raw philosophy and forged it into a modern, regulated sport where every style is welcome and every warrior is invited to find their limit.

The Complete Test: Weaponry to Warfare

Juego Todo is the most comprehensive combat platform because it demands mastery of every range and the seamless transition between them:

  • Double Baston (Sticks): Focuses on high-speed coordination, flow, and ambidextrous striking.
  • Single Stick: Focuses on precision, power, and the entry into the clinch.
  • Empty Hands: The ultimate transition. All strikes, takedowns, and submissions are welcomed.

Safety & Professionalism

We honor the warrior spirit without sacrificing the athlete. Amateur competitors are equipped with specialized headgear, gloves, shin guards, and mouthguards to ensure a high-level competitive environment with managed risk.

The Crucible: Three Rounds in the Cage

Matches are designed to peel back the layers of a fighter's skill under intense pressure:

1. Round 1 (Double Sticks): High-speed weaponry. All techniques, strikes, and takedowns are permitted (excluding submissions).

2. Round 2 (Single Stick): The intensity ramps up. All techniques from Round 1 are included, now adding the full range of submissions.

3. Round 3 (The Finale): The "Anything Goes" spectrum. From sticks to empty hands all strikes, takedowns, and submissions are legal.
