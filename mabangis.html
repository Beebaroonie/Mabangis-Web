MABANGIS: HIGH INTENSITY COMBAT & SPORTS SCIENCE

Mabangis is a high intensity combat system that bridges the gap between tactical execution and sports level conditioning. It is a seamless curriculum that synchronizes weaponry and empty hand combatives, designed to maintain a relentless pace while delivering decisive tactical finishes.

Core Philosophy

Unlike traditional combat systems that focus on static or specialized scenarios, Mabangis operates at a sports level pace. We actively condition the cardio, footwork, and reflexes alongside developing the mental state required to finish the job.

The Fusion of Combat and Science

What makes Mabangis unique is this fusion of combatives and sports science. By subjecting tactical work to high intensity pressure, the system naturally filters out unrealistic techniques. This synergy ensures that every movement is synchronized with the practitioner's physicality—providing a clear, unvarnished look at what they truly need to master.

Tactical Neutralization

Unlike combat sports, which trade blows for points, Mabangis eliminates the exchange in real world scenarios. We prioritize the immediate neutralization of the threat, and because specialized fitness training is built into the core of the curriculum, the practitioner is never limited by their own engine.

Sport Implementation & Safety Standard

As an ambassador for Juego Todo USA, the Mabangis curriculum utilized in competitive formats is specifically adapted for the arena. To align with international sporting standards and ensure practitioner safety, all lethal techniques and high risk tactical finishes are strictly excluded from the sport regulated syllabus.
